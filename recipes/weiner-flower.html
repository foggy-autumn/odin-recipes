Description

Create a pretty protein by cutting a hot dog into a flower!

Source: Just Bento

Ingredients

  • Any flexible weiner such as Hot dogs
  • Small sharp knife
  • Toothpicks

Directions

  1. Cut the weiner in half lengthwise
  2. Place one half cut-side down so that it curves away from you
  3. Make small cuts along the edge taking care not to cut all the way through to the other edge.
  4. Cut a slice of another weiner. Make criss-cross slices along the top edge.
  5. Bring a small amount of water to a boil in a pan.
  6. The Criss-cross cut weiner slice will act as the center of the flower. Take the long hot dog slice and wrap it around the flower center so that the slices you made open up as it encircles the center. Trim the excess so it wraps entirely around the circumference of the center slice. Hold this shape by pinning it closed with one toothpick, and pierce through the center slice with another toothpick.
  7. Boil the weiner flower for a few minutes to set the shape. When the cuts in the center piece have opened up a bit, you may remove it from the water.
  8. You leave the flower as-is, or add color by panfrying it for a few minutes!
