Description

The perfect Halloween lunch! Make Jack O'Lanterns using rice, smoked salmon, and nori seaweed!

Source: Japan Up Magazine

Ingredients

  • 20g x 2 of Smoked Salmon
  • 80g x 2 of Sushi Rice
  • A piece of Nori Seaweed

Directions

  1. Lay Salmon down on a sheet of plastic wrap, then place rice on top. Fold up the ends of the plastic wrap and twist tightly to create a ball shape
  2. Use scissors to cut shapes from the Nori Seaweed sheet. Cut a pair of Triangles for the eyes and small strips for the mouth.
  3. Position Rice ball in bento box. Carefully place Nori pieces onto the rice ball to create the face. You can use tweezers for precision!
  4. Repeat steps to create the second Jack O'Lantern
